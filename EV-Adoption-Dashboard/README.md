# Electric Vehicle Adoption, Growth & Policy Insights (2016–2023)

## Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes Electric Vehicle (EV) adoption trends from 2016 to 2023, focusing on growth acceleration, manufacturer performance, geographic concentration, and policy eligibility impact.

The goal of this analysis is to evaluate long-term EV market expansion and identify key drivers influencing adoption.

## Key Metrics

- **Total EVs:** 159K

- **Latest Year EV Count:** 59K

- **YoY Growth (Latest Year):** 436%

- **EV CAGR (2016–2023):** 41.9%

- **BEV Market Share:** 80.9%

## Key Insights

- EV adoption grew at a 41.9% CAGR, indicating sustained long-term growth.

- The latest year shows significant acceleration with 436% YoY growth.

- BEVs now account for approximately 81% of the total EV fleet, reflecting a market shift away from PHEVs.

- Nearly half of EV registrations are concentrated in a single county.

- Tesla leads manufacturer adoption by a substantial margin.

- Approximately 58% of EVs qualify under CAFV eligibility criteria.
